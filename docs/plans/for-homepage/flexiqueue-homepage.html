Lifecycle

How a session works.

Every client interaction follows a clear, trackable lifecycle from start to finish.

01
Token issued

A client receives a token — printed, scanned, or via QR code. Bound to a session by triage staff.

02
Called to station

Staff calls the next token. Display boards update instantly. TTS announces the number aloud.

03
Served or transferred

Client is served at the window. Staff can transfer, override, add notes, or escalate as needed.

04
Completed & logged

Session marked complete. Data feeds into analytics, logs, and throughput reports.

No login needed
