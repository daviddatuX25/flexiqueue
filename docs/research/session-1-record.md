## 3. System Demo (Initial Version)

*Aligned with manuscript Ch2, System Design: "interface mockups for administration, staff stations, and beneficiary informants presented for feedback until validated."*

### 3.1 Admin / Configuration

- **Login** — Researcher demonstrated system access.
- **Program setup** — Creation of stations; assignment of staff to stations; creation of **tracks**.
  - **Track** was explained (e.g. **regular track** = normal flow of a program; other tracks can be created, e.g. shortcut track or irregular-flow tracks).
- **Tokens** — Bulk print feature; use of tokens in the queue process.
- **Program settings** — Ma'am La Arni was given a brief look at the program settings page.

**Decision during demo:** Ma'am La Arni suggested it would be better if the researcher **communicates with MSWDO IT personnel** for technical/configuration details. Researcher agreed; this is noted for follow-up (see Section 5).

### 3.2 Program-Day Simulation (Queue Management)

- **Staff stations** — Staff should be on their stations and logged in on their interfaces.
- **Triage** — Explained as the interface where a **client is registered to start a process**. Tokens were explained as **essential for preserving beneficiary privacy** throughout the queue process.
- **Token scan** — Researcher showed a **recording** of staff scanning a token; process starts for that token; in the staff interface the session appears as **"waiting."**
- **Call next** — Staff with the queue clicks **"Call next"**; researcher demonstrated **Track A only** (normal flow: no overrides, no no-show demos).
- **Flow to completion** — Demonstrated staff clicking **"Send to next station"**; process passes to the next station until the process ends.
- **Informant desk (public-facing)** — Demonstrated:
  - Display of queue process / "now serving" information.
  - **Scan feature** so beneficiaries/clients can see **where their progress currently is**.

### 3.3 Session End

- Session was concluded after the informant-desk demo.
- No formal hands-on trial by staff in this session; focus was interview + demo with MSWDO Head.
