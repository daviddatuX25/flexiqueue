\# 🧠 FIRST — Confirmed Rules (From Your Notes + Guide)



\### 📄 Formatting (Critical — Panel checks this first)



\* Font: Times New Roman

\* Main body: \*\*Size 12\*\*

\* Preliminaries: \*\*Size 8 (per lecture note)\*\*

\* Spacing: Double spacing

\* Paragraph: 0.5 inch indent

\* Alignment: Justified

\* Chapter pages numbering: \*\*Top Right\*\*

\* Preliminaries numbering: \*\*Bottom Center\*\*



These match the official manuscript standards (margins, spacing, justification, etc.).
