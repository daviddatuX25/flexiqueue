8. Research Design

The study uses a descriptive research design and a developmental research design.

According to Siedlecki (2020), the goal of descriptive studies is to describe individuals, events, or conditions as they occur in nature. The researchers do not modify any of the variables, instead, they simply describe the sample and/or the variables. Although descriptive research can investigate several variables, it is the only design that can investigate only one variable.

Define descriptive developmental research design and mention the source of your literature.

Discuss how descriptive developmental research applies to your study.
